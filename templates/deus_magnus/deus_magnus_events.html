Deus Magnus at the Big 5 Construct Nigeria 2024: Leading the
Conversation on Innovation and Sustainable Development

Introduction

The 4th edition of the Big 5 Construct Nigeria was held at the Landmark Centre in Lagos from September 24-26, 2024. As Nigeria’s largest construction industry event, the exhibition brought together over 9,000 professionals and 150 exhibitors from more than 20 countries, showcasing cutting-edge technologies and products that are set to reshape Nigeria’s $128 billion construction sector. This year’s event focused on key themes such as innovation, sustainability, and technology, creating the perfect environment for Deus Magnus to highlight its commitment to excellence and forward-thinking solutions in construction and real estate development.

Our Presence at the Event

Deus Magnus’s participation in the event was marked by active engagement in several of the 25 CPD-certified Big 5 Talks, where industry experts and thought leaders discussed emerging trends and opportunities in Nigeria’s construction industry. Our team took this opportunity to connect with other professionals, learn about groundbreaking innovations, and explore partnerships that align with our strategic goals of sustainable development and premium service delivery. Key highlights included discussions on the adoption of Building Information Modeling (BIM), the impact of smart home technologies, and the role of sustainable building practices in shaping the future of the Nigerian construction landscape. Through these conversations, we were able to reinforce our position as a key player in driving the integration of these technologies and practices into the local industry.

Showcasing Our Commitment to Innovation

At Deus Magnus, we believe that innovation is at the core of delivering exceptional projects. The Big 5 Construct Nigeria provided a platform to demonstrate how our approach to construction and facility management is aligned with global best practices. Our team shared insights on the latest sustainable construction methods and innovative materials that are redefining the luxury real estate market in Nigeria. Our presence at the event not only positioned Deus Magnus as a forward-thinking leader but also highlighted our ability to deliver solutions that meet the evolving needs of our high-net-worth clients and stakeholders.

Future Outlook

The Big 5 Construct Nigeria 2024 marked a significant milestone in Nigeria’s construction industry, with the government’s infrastructure development strategies set to boost growth at a CAGR of 2.4% between 2024 and 2028. With increased public and private investment in the housing sector, Deus Magnus is well-placed to capitalize on these opportunities, leveraging our expertise in real estate development, construction services, and facility management. Moving forward, we remain committed to participating in similar industry events that allow us to stay ahead of the curve, forge valuable partnerships, and deliver excellence in every project we undertake.
